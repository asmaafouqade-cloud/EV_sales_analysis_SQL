
    Key insights
Note : These insights reflect this dataset only and do not necessarily represent real-world trends

- Canada records the highest EV sales in the dataset
- Hyundai and Kia appear as the leading brands in terms of market presence
- Lower-priced EV brands tend to sell the most 
- Europe shows the highest EV adoption rates among regions
- Governments incentives are associated with higher EV adoption rates
- SUVs show the greatest average CO2 reduction, while buses show the lowest
